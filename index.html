Privacy Policy

PilotAero (“we”, “us”, or “our”) provides study tools for pilots. This privacy notice explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices. It applies to our iOS app and related services.

Data We Collect

  • Account data (if you sign in): email address or Apple ID identifier and display name.
  • Profile photo (optional): only the single photo you select is uploaded; the app cannot browse your library without your selection.
  • Usage & diagnostics: app actions, device/OS info, and crash data to improve reliability and content.
  • Subscription status: purchase and renewal information from Apple’s in‑app purchase system (no payment card details stored by us).
  • Support: messages you send to our support email.

Why We Use Your Data

  • Provide core features (progress, saved preferences, content access).
  • Authenticate accounts, secure the service, and prevent abuse.
  • Measure performance and improve the app and learning content.
  • Validate active subscriptions and restore purchases.
  • Respond to support requests.

Photos Permission

When you add a profile picture, iOS may show “Private Access to Photos”. We only receive the specific photo you choose. The app includes NSPhotoLibraryUsageDescription explaining this purpose.

Retention

  • Account data is kept while your account is active.
  • Diagnostics/analytics are retained for a limited period consistent with platform defaults.
  • You can request deletion at any time (see below).

Your Choices

  • Access/Update data in‑app where available.
  • Delete account & dataDelete account & data: You can delete your account directly from the Profile page inside the app. All associated data will be removed. Alternatively, you may also email support@pilotaero.org from the same address used in the app..
  • Permissions: you can revoke Photos access from iOS Settings at any time.

Children

The service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Security

We use industry‑standard safeguards (encrypted transport, restricted access). No method is 100% secure, and we continually improve protections.

Changes & Contact

البيانات التي نجمعها

  • بيانات الحساب (إن سجّلت الدخول): البريد الإلكتروني أو معرّف Apple والاسم المعروض.

  • الاشتراكات: حالة الشراء والتجديد من Apple (لا نحتفظ ببيانات البطاقات).
  • الدعم: رسائلك إلى بريدنا.

أغراض الاستخدام

  • تشغيل الميزات الأساسية وحفظ الإعدادات.
  • تأمين الحسابات ومنع إساءة الاستخدام.

  • التحقق من الاشتراكات واستعادتها.
  • الرد على طلبات الدعم.

إذن الصور

عند إضافة صورة، قد يعرض iOS نافذة “وصول خاص للصور”. نتلقى فقط الصورة التي اخترتها. يتضمن التطبيق وصف الإذن NSPhotoLibraryUsageDescription.

الاحتفاظ بالبيانات

  • تُحفظ بيانات التشخيص لفترة محدودة وفق إعدادات المنصّة.
  • يمكنك طلب الحذف في أي وقت.

حقوقك

  • طلب حذف الحساب والبيانات عبر البريد: support@pilotaero.org.
  • إلغاء إذن الصور من إعدادات iOS.
