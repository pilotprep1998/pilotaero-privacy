Privacy Policy

PilotAero (“we”, “us”, or “our”) provides study and training tools for pilots. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. It applies to our iOS app and related services.

Data We Collect

  • Account & Authentication (if you sign in): email address, Apple ID identifier, basic profile. Used to create and secure your account.
  • Profile Photo (optional): if you choose a picture from your Photos, it is uploaded to our storage and cached on your device. We request access at the moment you pick a photo; the app cannot browse your library without your selection.
  • App Usage & Analytics: device model, OS version, in‑app actions, and diagnostics (e.g., crashes). We use Firebase Analytics / GoogleAppMeasurement to improve reliability and content.
  • Subscriptions & Purchases: transaction identifiers and status via Apple’s in‑app purchase system (no card numbers are stored by us).
  • Support: messages you send to support@pilotaero.org.

How We Use Data

  • Provide core features (progress, question banks, saved preferences).
  • Authenticate, prevent abuse, and keep the service secure.
  • Measure performance and improve content and UX.
  • Process and validate subscriptions.
  • Respond to support requests.

Third‑Party Services

  • Firebase (Analytics/Crash/Storage) — Google LLC.
  • Apple (Sign in with Apple, App Store payments).
  • Optional marketing/attribution such as Google Ads conversion if enabled; we do not sell personal data.

Photos Permission

When you add a profile picture, iOS may show a “Private Access to Photos” panel. We only receive the photo you select. The app’s NSPhotoLibraryUsageDescription explains this purpose.

Data Retention

  • Account data is kept while your account is active.
  • Analytics and logs are retained for a limited period consistent with their providers’ defaults.
  • You may request deletion at any time (see “Your Choices”).

Your Choices & Rights

  • Access / Update: edit your info inside the app where available.
  • Delete Account & Data: email support@pilotaero.org from the same address used in the app and request deletion; we’ll confirm and erase associated personal data unless we must retain it to meet legal or fraud‑prevention obligations.
  • Opt‑out of Analytics: you can limit ad/analytics tracking in iOS settings (Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking).

Children

PilotAero is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided data, contact us to remove it.

Security

We use industry‑standard measures (encryption in transit, restricted access). No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; we continually improve safeguards.

International Transfers

Data may be processed in countries where our providers operate (e.g., the United States). We rely on appropriate safeguards provided by those processors.
